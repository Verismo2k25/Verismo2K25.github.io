JOURNEY TO THE TECH VERSE

VERISMO meaning 'Realism' is an annual Techno-Cultural programme for encouraging and promoting innovations from all Students in the state . Verismo has been conducted by the MCA DEPARTMENT OF LOURDES MATHA COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

VERISMO is a collaborative effort of all students and teachers of MCA DEPARTMENT. We thank all those who take their effort to bring this program to a great success. Technology is an ever-evolving field that shapes our world in profound ways. From the dawn of the digital age to today's advancements in artificial intelligence, technology has revolutionized how we live, work, and connect with each other.
